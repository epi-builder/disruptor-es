| T-07-06 | Denial of Service | metrics series cardinality | mitigate | Metric catalog limits labels to aggregate/outcome/reason/shard/projector/topic. |
| T-07-07 | Repudiation | command tracing | mitigate | Spans include command/correlation/causation IDs as trace fields, not metric labels. |
</threat_model>
